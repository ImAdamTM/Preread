# Preread — Feature Breakdown

## The Pitch

All the things you love to read. In one place. Ready whenever you are.

Preread is your personal article reader. Add websites and publications you love, and Preread visits them on your behalf — preparing full articles so they're ready before you are. On a plane, underground, or just away from WiFi — your reading is always there, beautifully presented and distraction-free.

Unlike social media feeds full of noise, Preread lets you curate exactly what you want to read. No algorithm deciding what's important. No distractions competing for your attention. Just the articles you chose, from the sources you trust, beautifully presented and waiting for you.

---

## Core Value Propositions

### Your Personal Reader
Preread reads on your behalf. It quietly visits your favourite sites in the background and prepares full articles — not just headlines — so they're ready when you are. When you open the app, everything is already there. No loading spinners, no "check your connection" messages. Your reading library stays current without you lifting a finger.

### Discover New Interests
Browse a curated directory of 500+ sources across 55+ topics and countries — from world news and science to food, architecture, photography, and beyond. Stumble into new interests you didn't know you had.

### The Antidote to Doomscrolling
Social media buries the things you care about under reams of irrelevant noise. Preread is the opposite — a calm, focused space where every article is something you chose. It's a reading experience designed for learning and enjoyment, not engagement metrics.

### Offline by Default
Every article is fully cached — text, images, everything — stored on your device. Flight mode, subway, camping in the middle of nowhere — your articles work exactly the same. Never worry about not having anything to read before boarding a plane again.

### A Clean, Reading-First Experience
Preread presents articles in a beautifully typeset reader focused entirely on the writing and images. Customisable fonts, adjustable text sizes, and a calm, focused layout. Just you and the article. Reading the way it should be.

## Feature Catalogue

### Reading Experience
- **Reading-first experience** — Articles are presented in a clean, focused reader view with beautiful typography. Just the writing and images — nothing else
- **Full articles, not just headlines** — Preread prepares complete articles with images, so they're ready to read in full
- **Customisable typography** — Choose from System, Georgia, New York, or Palatino fonts. Adjust text size from 14pt to 24pt with a smooth slider
- **Dark & Light mode** — Follows system appearance or set per-app. The reader adapts seamlessly
- **Reading time estimates** — Every article shows an estimated reading time so you can pick the right article for the moment
- **Image lightbox** — Tap any image to view full-screen with pinch-to-zoom and double-tap zoom
- **Full-page mode** — For sites with complex layouts, cache the entire styled page for a faithful offline experience

### Discovering Sources
- **Curated topic directory** — Browse 500+ sources across 55+ topics and countries: World News, Tech, AI, Science, Space, Business, Sports, Health, Film & TV, Music, Gaming, Food, Travel, Books, Fashion, Photography, DIY, Cars, Celebrity & Pop Culture, Home & Design, Environment, History, and many more
- **Browse by country** — Sources organised by 20+ countries, from the US and UK to Japan, Brazil, India, and beyond
- **Search for anything** — Type a keyword like "space" or "cooking" and instantly find matching publications from the built-in directory
- **Add any website** — Paste any URL and Preread auto-discovers the feed. Works with website homepages, blog URLs, and direct feed links
- **Save single pages** — Not everything has a feed. Paste any URL to cache a single webpage for offline reading

### Home Screen
- **Personalised home view** — "Preread for you" shows your sources with article counts, reading times, and unread indicators
- **Latest articles carousel** — A horizontal carousel of the newest articles across all your sources, evenly balanced
- **Saved articles section** — Quick access to your bookmarked articles at the top of the home screen
- **Collapsible source sections** — Expand or collapse individual sources to focus on what matters right now
- **Pull-to-refresh** — Manually refresh everything with a pull gesture

### Article Management
- **Save articles** — Bookmark any article to your Saved collection for easy access later
- **Read/unread tracking** — Articles are marked as read when opened. Swipe to toggle read status
- **Swipe actions** — Quick gestures to mark read/unread, save, re-fetch, or remove articles
- **Context menus** — Long-press for share, open in Safari, re-fetch, save, or delete
- **Search** — Find articles within a source or across your saved collection
- **Share original links** — Share buttons always link back to the original article on the publisher's website
- **Open in Safari** — Jump to the original page anytime

### Set It and Forget It
- **Background refresh** — Preread automatically checks for new articles in the background, so they're ready when you open the app
- **Background caching** — Full articles are fetched and cached during longer background tasks while you're on WiFi
- **WiFi-only option** — Restrict background fetching to WiFi to save mobile data
- **Three sync modes** — Automatic (set and forget), On Open (refreshes when you launch), or Manual (only when you ask)
- **Per-source settings** — Configure cache level, fetch frequency, and layout preferences for each source individually
- **Reorder your sources** — Drag and drop to arrange sources in the order that matters to you

### Widgets
- **Home screen widgets** — Small, medium, and large widgets showing your latest articles with thumbnails
- **Lock screen widgets** — Glanceable article previews right on your lock screen
- **Per-source widgets** — Show articles from a specific source or all sources
- **Tap to read** — Tap any widget article to jump straight into the reader

### Apple Watch
- **Watch companion app** — View your latest articles on your wrist
- **Automatic sync** — Articles sync from your iPhone whenever it updates
- **Watch widgets** — Article previews for watch complications

### Share Extension
- **Share from anywhere** — Use the iOS share sheet from Safari, Chrome, or any app to send a URL to Preread
- **Seamless handoff** — The URL opens directly in Preread for feed discovery or page saving

### Shortcuts & Siri
- **Siri voice commands** — "Open [source] in Preread" or "Read [source]"
- **Home screen shortcuts** — Create quick-access shortcuts to jump to specific sources
- **Deep links** — URL scheme support for automation and integration with Shortcuts app

### iPad Support
- **Split view** — Sidebar with sources, detail pane with the reader. Full use of iPad screen real estate
- **Landscape & portrait** — Responsive layouts for any orientation

### Design & Polish
- **Custom Gabarito typeface** — A distinctive, modern typeface used throughout the app
- **Signature gradient** — A teal-to-purple accent gradient across buttons, badges, and highlights
- **Haptic feedback** — Subtle haptics on refresh, toggles, sliders, and confirmations
- **Smooth animations** — Zoom transitions, scroll-linked effects, and matched geometry animations
- **Non-intrusive notifications** — Toast-style feedback for saves, copies, and errors
- **Auto-generated avatars** — Sources without icons get colourful gradient letter avatars
- **Accessibility** — Respects Dynamic Type, Reduce Motion, and system accessibility settings

### Storage & Data
- **Storage dashboard** — See exactly how much space each source uses, with a visual breakdown
- **Configurable article limits** — Choose 10, 15, 25, or 50 articles per source
- **Low storage warnings** — Alerts when device storage is running low
- **Automatic cleanup** — Orphaned files, duplicates, and stale data are cleaned up on launch
- **Efficient caching** — Shared asset deduplication keeps storage usage minimal
